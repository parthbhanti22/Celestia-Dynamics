  • THERMAL CONDUCTIVITY SIMULATION IN 1D MATERIALS

    Explore the fascinating world of thermal conductivity simulation for 1D materials. Unlike bulk materials, heat transfer in 1D materials—such as carbon nanotubes and nanowires—is governed by unique quantum and phonon transport effects. Our simulation allows you to analyze how heat propagates in these nanoscale structures, accounting for factors like phonon scattering, boundary effects, and ballistic transport. By adjusting material properties and environmental conditions, you can gain insights into the thermal behavior of 1D systems, aiding research in nanoelectronics, thermoelectric applications, and advanced thermal management solutions.
